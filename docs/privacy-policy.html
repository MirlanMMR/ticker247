Privacy Policy — Ticker 24/7

Last updated: June 20, 2025

Ticker 24/7 ("the App") is developed by MMR Lab. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects minimal data necessary to provide its services:

  • Google Account (optional): If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address via Firebase Authentication, used only to personalize your news feed.
  • Device language and region: Used to select the appropriate news pool (Russian, English, Spanish, or Portuguese).
  • Firebase usage data: The App uses Firebase Realtime Database to deliver news content. Standard Firebase analytics may collect anonymous usage statistics.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your location.
  • We do not collect contacts, photos, or any files from your device.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — news database and authentication
  • Google Sign-In — optional account login
  • YouTube — viral video content in the feed

4. Guest Mode

You may use the App without signing in. In guest mode, no account information is collected or stored.

5. Notifications

The App sends push notifications about breaking news and market updates. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

6. Data Retention

If you sign in with Google, your authentication data is stored in Firebase and can be deleted at any time by contacting us or uninstalling the App.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.

9. Disclaimer

Ticker 24/7 is a news aggregator. We are not the authors of the content and are not responsible for its accuracy, completeness, or timeliness. All rights to the content belong to the original sources. By using the App, you agree to access third-party content at your own discretion.
